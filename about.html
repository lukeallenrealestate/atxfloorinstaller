About ATX Floor Installer

ATX Floor Installer is a locally owned and operated flooring company serving homeowners and businesses across Greater Austin, TX. With more than 15 years of hands-on experience and over 500 floors installed, we bring the expertise, craftsmanship, and attention to detail that Central Texas homeowners deserve.

Our Story

We started ATX Floor Installer with a simple belief: every homeowner in Austin deserves access to honest, skilled flooring professionals who show up on time, do quality work, and stand behind it. What began as a small crew handling hardwood installations in Westlake Hills and South Austin has grown into one of the most trusted flooring teams in the Greater Austin area.

Over the years, we have installed and refinished floors in homes of every size — from 400-square-foot condos in downtown Austin to 5,000-square-foot estates in Lakeway and Dripping Springs. Every project gets the same level of care, because we know your floors are the foundation of your home.

What We Do

We specialize in four core flooring services:

  • Hardwood floor installation — solid and engineered hardwood, including oak, maple, walnut, hickory, and more
  • Luxury vinyl plank (LVP) — waterproof, pet-friendly, and built for Austin's humidity
  • Tile installation — porcelain, ceramic, and natural stone for kitchens, bathrooms, entryways, and outdoor spaces
  • Hardwood refinishing — sanding, staining, and sealing to restore the natural beauty of existing hardwood floors

We also handle subfloor preparation, old flooring removal, transitions, and trim work. When you hire ATX Floor Installer, we manage the full scope of work from start to finish.

Our Values

Craftsmanship First

We measure twice, cut once, and never cut corners. Our installers are trained professionals with years of field experience — not subcontracted day laborers. Every plank, every tile, every seam is laid with precision because we treat your home like our own.

Honest & Transparent

We provide detailed written estimates before any work begins. No hidden fees, no surprise charges, no pressure sales tactics. We explain your options, help you understand the tradeoffs, and let you decide what is best for your home and budget.

Clean & Respectful

We protect your furniture, contain dust, and leave your home cleaner than we found it. Our crews are punctual, courteous, and respectful of your space. We know you are living in your home during the install, and we work hard to minimize disruption.

Satisfaction Guaranteed

We stand behind every installation with a workmanship guarantee. If something is not right, we make it right — period. Our reputation in the Austin community is built on doing excellent work and taking care of our customers long after the job is done.

Our Team

Our team includes experienced flooring installers, each with specialized skills in hardwood, LVP, tile, or refinishing. Every crew member is trained in proper subfloor assessment, moisture testing, material acclimation, and manufacturer-recommended installation techniques. We invest in ongoing training to stay current with new products, methods, and industry best practices.

We are fully licensed and insured, giving you peace of mind that your home and your investment are protected throughout the entire project.
