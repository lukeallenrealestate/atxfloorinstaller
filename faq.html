Flooring installation costs in Austin typically range from $3–$6 per square foot for labor, depending on the material and complexity of the job. Here are general installed price ranges:

  • Hardwood: $6–$12 per sq ft installed
  • Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP): $4–$8 per sq ft installed
  • Tile: $7–$15 per sq ft installed
  • Hardwood refinishing: $3–$6 per sq ft

These ranges include materials and labor. We provide free on-site estimates with exact pricing tailored to your project — no guesswork, no surprises.

Timeline depends on the size of the project and the type of flooring:

  • Single room (up to 300 sq ft): 1–2 days
  • Whole home (1,000–2,000 sq ft): 3–5 days
  • Hardwood refinishing: 3–5 days including dry time

We provide a detailed timeline before work begins so you know exactly what to expect. Subfloor prep, old flooring removal, and complex layouts may add time.

Luxury vinyl plank (LVP) is the top choice for pet owners in Austin. It is 100% waterproof, scratch-resistant, and easy to clean. Accidents wipe right up, and claws do not damage the surface the way they can on hardwood.

Tile is another excellent pet-friendly option, especially for entryways and mudrooms. If you prefer the look of hardwood, we recommend engineered hardwood with a durable aluminum-oxide finish and harder wood species like hickory or white oak.

It depends on your priorities:

  • LVP advantages: Lower cost, waterproof, more scratch-resistant, easier to maintain, faster installation
  • Hardwood advantages: Timeless beauty, increases home value, can be refinished multiple times, lasts 50+ years

For families with young kids and pets, LVP is often the practical choice. For homeowners wanting long-term value and a classic look, hardwood is hard to beat. We help you weigh the pros and cons based on your specific home and lifestyle.

Subfloor preparation ensures the surface beneath your new flooring is level, clean, dry, and structurally sound. This step is critical for a long-lasting installation. Common subfloor prep includes:

  • Self-leveling compound for uneven concrete
  • Moisture barriers for slab foundations
  • Plywood underlayment replacement
  • Removal of old adhesive, staples, or tack strips

Skipping subfloor prep leads to squeaks, gaps, and premature wear. We assess your subfloor during the free estimate and include any needed prep in our quote.

Here is a simple checklist to prepare:

  • Remove personal items, fragile decor, and electronics from the work area
  • Clear closets if floors will be installed inside them
  • Ensure clear access to all rooms being worked on
  • Secure pets in a separate area during installation
  • Confirm parking and entry access for our crew and materials

We handle furniture moving and old flooring removal as part of the job. We will walk you through everything during the estimate so there are no surprises on install day.

Engineered hardwood is the top choice for Austin because of our humidity swings. Unlike solid hardwood, engineered planks have a plywood core that resists expansion and contraction. White oak and hickory are the most popular species — both are hard, durable, and handle Austin's climate well. We help you select the right product based on your home's specific conditions, including slab vs. pier-and-beam foundations.

Yes. We refinish solid hardwood and many engineered hardwood floors (if the wear layer is thick enough). The process includes:

  • Sanding down to bare wood
  • Applying your choice of stain color (or leaving natural)
  • Sealing with a durable polyurethane finish (water-based or oil-based)

Most refinishing projects take 3–5 days including dry time. The results are dramatic — worn, scratched floors look brand new.
