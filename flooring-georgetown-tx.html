Trusted Flooring Services in Georgetown, TX

Georgetown has a personality all its own. It is the seat of Williamson County, home to Southwestern University, and consistently ranked among the most livable small cities in America. The historic downtown square, with its beautifully restored limestone courthouse, sets the tone for a community that values craftsmanship, heritage, and quality of life. ATX Floor Installer is honored to serve Georgetown homeowners with flooring installation and refinishing services that reflect those same values.

Georgetown's housing landscape is remarkably diverse. The city is home to Sun City, one of the largest active-adult communities in Texas, where retirees are constantly updating and personalizing their homes. Berry Creek and Cimarron Hills offer upscale golf-course living. The historic downtown district features charming older homes with original hardwood and unique architectural details. And new master-planned communities are expanding the city's footprint to the east and south. We serve all of these areas with equal dedication.

Flooring for Georgetown's Active-Adult Communities

Sun City is a major part of Georgetown's identity, and flooring needs in this 55-and-older community have their own distinct character. Residents here are often downsizing from larger homes and want flooring that is low-maintenance, comfortable underfoot, and visually appealing. Safety is also a priority, as smooth, level surfaces with minimal tripping hazards make daily living easier and more secure. We work with many Sun City homeowners to install LVP and hardwood with proper transitions, wide doorway clearances, and slip-resistant finishes that support active, independent living.

Beyond Sun City, Georgetown's retirement-friendly atmosphere extends to neighborhoods like The Oaks at San Gabriel and Georgetown Village, where similar priorities guide flooring decisions. Our team is patient, respectful, and accustomed to working in occupied homes where minimizing disruption is essential.

Hardwood Flooring Installation

Hardwood flooring pairs beautifully with Georgetown's traditional architecture. In the historic homes near the downtown square, hardwood complements original millwork, high ceilings, and period details. In newer communities like Berry Creek and Riverview Estates, hardwood creates a refined look that elevates the builder-standard finishes. We install solid and engineered hardwood in white oak, red oak, hickory, and specialty species. Our installation teams pay close attention to acclimation requirements, especially important in Georgetown where summer heat and winter dry spells can affect wood moisture content.

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

LVP has become the top flooring choice in Georgetown across all demographics. Sun City residents love it for its durability and easy maintenance. Young families in Estrella Crossing and Saddlecreek appreciate its waterproof qualities and kid-friendly resilience. Homeowners in Berry Creek value the premium wood-look options that mimic the appearance of exotic hardwoods at a fraction of the price. LVP works on any subfloor type and installs quickly with minimal disruption, which is especially appealing for Georgetown homeowners who prefer a fast turnaround.

Tile Installation

Georgetown's Texas Hill Country setting inspires many homeowners to choose natural stone and stone-look tile that connects their interiors to the surrounding landscape. Travertine, limestone-look porcelain, and warm-toned ceramics are popular selections. In Sun City, tile is frequently chosen for bathrooms and kitchen areas where water resistance matters most. We also install decorative tile in entryways, fireplace surrounds, and outdoor living spaces. Our tile installers ensure perfectly level surfaces and consistent grout joints regardless of the complexity of the layout.

Hardwood Refinishing

Georgetown's historic homes near the downtown square and along University Avenue often contain original hardwood floors with decades of character. These floors may show scratches, water stains, and worn finish, but the wood itself is often in excellent condition. Our refinishing service strips away the old finish, repairs any damage, applies your chosen stain, and seals the wood with a professional-grade coating. The result preserves the historic charm of your Georgetown home while giving the floors a fresh, updated appearance.
