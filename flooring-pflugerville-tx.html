Affordable Flooring Upgrades for Pflugerville Homes

Pflugerville is one of the fastest-growing cities in the Austin metro, and that growth shows no signs of slowing down. New subdivisions are appearing along SH 130 and Pflugerville Parkway, while established neighborhoods like Blackhawk, Falcon Pointe, and Spring Trails continue to mature. Whether you just moved into a brand-new home and want to swap out the builder-grade flooring, or you have lived in Pflugerville for years and your existing floors are ready for an upgrade, ATX Floor Installer is here to help with professional installation at competitive prices.

We believe that quality flooring should be accessible to everyone, not just luxury homeowners. Pflugerville families work hard, and they deserve floors that look great, perform well, and fit within a reasonable budget. That philosophy drives everything we do, from helping you select the right materials to providing transparent pricing with no hidden costs.

New Construction Flooring Upgrades

Many of Pflugerville's newest homes come with basic flooring packages that builders install to keep construction costs down. Thin carpet in the bedrooms, entry-level tile in the bathrooms, and cheap vinyl in the kitchen are common in homes delivered by production builders. While these floors serve their purpose initially, they wear out quickly and rarely reflect the homeowner's personal style.

We work with many Pflugerville homeowners who want to upgrade their new home's flooring within the first year or two of ownership. In some cases, homeowners choose to upgrade before even moving in, turning the house into a home from day one. Popular upgrades include replacing carpet with LVP throughout the main level, installing hardwood in the living areas, and upgrading bathroom tile to a more premium porcelain or natural stone.

Hardwood Flooring Installation

Hardwood flooring elevates any Pflugerville home from ordinary to exceptional. We specialize in engineered hardwood installation over concrete slab foundations, which is the standard construction method in Pflugerville subdivisions. Engineered hardwood offers the beauty and feel of solid wood with superior dimensional stability that handles the temperature and humidity fluctuations common in Central Texas. Our most popular hardwood options for Pflugerville include white oak in natural and light stain finishes, which brighten living spaces and create a modern, airy atmosphere.

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

LVP is the most requested flooring product in Pflugerville, and it is easy to understand why. It combines stunning wood-look visuals with waterproof construction, scratch resistance, and easy maintenance at a price point that fits family budgets. For households in Blackhawk and Falcon Pointe with kids and pets, LVP is a no-brainer. It handles spills, muddy paw prints, and toy-car races across the living room without showing wear. We carry a wide range of LVP styles from light ash and blonde oak to rich walnut and rustic barnwood tones.

Tile Installation

Tile provides lasting durability in the wet areas of Pflugerville homes. We install porcelain, ceramic, and stone tile in bathrooms, kitchens, laundry rooms, and entryways. In new Pflugerville subdivisions where builder-installed tile has chipped, cracked, or simply does not match the homeowner's taste, we remove the old tile and install a fresh surface that transforms the space. Wood-look porcelain tile planks have gained significant popularity in Pflugerville because they offer the visual warmth of wood with tile's water resistance and durability.

Hardwood Refinishing

While Pflugerville skews toward newer construction, there are established pockets of the city with homes built in the 1990s and early 2000s that have original hardwood floors in need of attention. In neighborhoods along Pecan Street and near old downtown Pflugerville, refinishing worn hardwood can bring floors back to life for a fraction of replacement cost. Our dustless sanding system keeps your home clean during the process, and we offer a full spectrum of stain colors to match your updated interior design.
