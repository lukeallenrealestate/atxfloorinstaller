Quality Flooring for Round Rock's Growing Families

Round Rock has earned its reputation as one of the best places to live in Texas, and it is not hard to see why. Top-rated schools in the Round Rock ISD, a thriving local economy anchored by major employers, and a family-oriented culture make this city a magnet for homeowners who are building their lives in the Austin area. As families settle in and make these houses their own, flooring upgrades are among the first and most impactful improvements on the list. ATX Floor Installer has been a trusted partner for Round Rock homeowners looking to elevate their living spaces with professional flooring installation.

We work in neighborhoods throughout Round Rock, from the fairway-lined streets of Forest Creek to the newer homes in Teravista and the established communities of Round Rock West. Each area has its own character, and we tailor our recommendations to match the specific needs of each home and household.

Replacing Builder-Grade Flooring

A significant portion of Round Rock's housing stock consists of homes built between the mid-1990s and the present day. Many of these were delivered with builder-grade carpet, basic ceramic tile, and sheet vinyl that was never meant to last a lifetime. After five to fifteen years of family use, these floors start showing their age. Carpet fibers mat down and trap allergens. Entry-level tile chips and stains. Sheet vinyl peels at the edges and yellows with time.

Replacing these surfaces with quality hardwood, LVP, or upgraded tile is one of the smartest investments a Round Rock homeowner can make. Our team handles the entire process from removing old flooring to preparing subfloors and completing a professional installation that transforms the look and feel of your home.

Hardwood Flooring Installation

Hardwood floors give Round Rock homes a timeless quality that sets them apart from neighboring properties. In neighborhoods like Forest Creek and Cat Hollow, where homes tend to be spacious with open floor plans, hardwood creates a cohesive flow between living, dining, and kitchen areas. We install engineered hardwood over concrete slabs, which is the predominant foundation type in Round Rock, using glue-down or floating methods depending on the product and conditions. White oak, hickory, and acacia are among the top choices for Round Rock installations.

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

For Round Rock families looking for maximum durability at an attractive price, LVP delivers. It handles everything that daily life throws at it: pet claws, spilled drinks, muddy shoes from playing at Old Settlers Park, and high-traffic pathways between rooms. Modern LVP comes in an impressive range of wood-look designs with textures that mimic real grain patterns. Many Round Rock homeowners choose to install LVP throughout the entire main level of their home, creating a seamless look from entryway to kitchen to family room. It is also an excellent choice for basements and bonus rooms.

Tile Installation

Tile remains a staple in Round Rock homes, particularly in bathrooms, kitchens, and laundry rooms. We install everything from classic subway tile to modern large-format porcelain and wood-look tile planks. In Round Rock's newer subdivisions, wood-look porcelain tile has become increasingly popular because it combines the warmth of a wood aesthetic with the waterproof durability of tile. Our installers ensure level surfaces, consistent grout lines, and clean transitions to adjacent flooring materials.

Hardwood Refinishing

Homes in older Round Rock neighborhoods like Round Rock West and Chisholm Crossing often have hardwood floors that have dulled and scratched over the years. Rather than replacing them entirely, refinishing can restore these floors to like-new condition at a fraction of the cost. Our process involves sanding down to bare wood, applying your choice of stain color, and sealing with a commercial-grade finish. The result is a floor that looks freshly installed and can last another twenty years with proper care.
