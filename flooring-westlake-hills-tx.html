Premium Flooring for Westlake Hills Luxury Homes

Westlake Hills is one of the most sought-after residential communities in the Austin metro area, known for its rolling terrain, mature tree canopy, and stunning custom homes. Homeowners here expect nothing less than the finest when it comes to their interiors, and flooring is no exception. ATX Floor Installer is proud to serve the Westlake Hills community with flooring installation and refinishing services that match the caliber of these exceptional properties.

Whether you own a sprawling estate in Rob Roy or a beautifully appointed home in Davenport Ranch, our team delivers flooring solutions that complement the architectural character of your space. We work closely with homeowners, interior designers, and custom builders throughout Westlake Hills to ensure every installation meets the highest standards of quality and visual appeal.

Why Westlake Hills Homeowners Choose Us

The homes in Westlake Hills are unique. Many feature open floor plans with expansive living areas, gourmet kitchens, and grand entryways that demand flooring capable of making a lasting impression. Others sit on hillsides with panoramic views, where the interior finishes need to match the beauty seen through the windows. Our installers understand this context and bring the skill required to execute flawless installations in high-end residential settings.

From the established neighborhoods near Westlake Drive to the newer custom builds along Redbud Trail, we have completed projects across every corner of Westlake Hills. Our reputation in the community is built on precision, reliability, and the kind of attention to detail that luxury homeowners demand.

Hardwood Flooring Installation

Hardwood is the flooring of choice for many Westlake Hills residences. We install both solid and engineered hardwood, including premium species like white oak, walnut, hickory, and European oak. Wide-plank options are especially popular in the grand living rooms and master suites found throughout the Rob Roy and Davenport Ranch neighborhoods. Our team handles everything from subfloor preparation and moisture testing to precision layout and expert finishing, ensuring your hardwood floors will look stunning for decades.

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

Even in luxury homes, LVP has earned its place. Today's premium luxury vinyl plank products offer the authentic look of natural wood or stone with waterproof durability that makes them ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, mudrooms, and lower-level spaces. In Westlake Hills, where homes often feature walk-out basements and multi-level designs built into the hillside, LVP provides a practical yet beautiful flooring solution that holds up to the demands of everyday living.

Tile Installation

From imported porcelain in spa-like master bathrooms to natural stone in grand foyers, tile work is a hallmark of Westlake Hills interiors. We install ceramic, porcelain, marble, travertine, and slate tile with expert precision. Our team specializes in complex layouts including herringbone, chevron, and custom mosaic patterns that elevate the design of your home. Whether it is a kitchen backsplash renovation or a full bathroom remodel, our tile installations are built to last.

Hardwood Refinishing

Many of the established homes in Westlake Hills have original hardwood floors that have accumulated years of wear. Scratches from furniture, fading from sunlight, and general traffic patterns can diminish the beauty of even the finest wood. Our refinishing service includes dustless sanding, custom staining to match your design vision, and professional-grade polyurethane or oil finishes that restore your floors to their original glory. Refinishing is often more cost-effective than replacement and preserves the character of your home.
