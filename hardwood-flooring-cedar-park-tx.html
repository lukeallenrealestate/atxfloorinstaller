Hardwood Floor Installation in Cedar Park, TX

Cedar Park sits at the heart of Austin's northwest tech corridor, and its homeowners have high standards when it comes to their homes. ATX Floor Installer provides professional hardwood floor installation for Cedar Park residences, from the established neighborhoods of Buttercup Creek and Anderson Mill to newer communities like Twin Creeks, Ranch at Brushy Creek, and Whitestone Oaks. Whether you are replacing worn carpet in a 1990s-era home or upgrading a recent build, we deliver hardwood floors that transform your living spaces.

Cedar Park's Mix of Housing Stock

Cedar Park's real estate ranges from homes built in the early 1990s around the Buttercup Creek and Anderson Mill areas to brand-new construction in the communities west of 183A. This mix creates two distinct hardwood flooring scenarios that we see regularly:

Older Cedar Park homes (1990s-2010s): Many of these were built with wall-to-wall carpet, basic ceramic tile in wet areas, and sometimes small sections of prefinished hardwood in entryways or dining rooms. After 15 to 25 years, the carpet is matted and stained, the tile looks dated, and those small hardwood sections show their age. Homeowners in Buttercup Creek, Anderson Mill West, and the Cypress Canyon area are upgrading entire main floors to hardwood, creating a cohesive, premium look that brings these homes into the current market standard.

Newer Cedar Park homes (2015-present): Homes in Twin Creeks, Ranch at Brushy Creek, and Whitestone Oaks were typically delivered with a mix of builder-grade LVP or laminate and carpet. While functional, these materials lack the character and long-term value of real hardwood. Many owners of these newer homes upgrade to engineered hardwood within the first few years, often before the builder warranty expires.

Best Hardwood Options for Cedar Park Homes

Cedar Park homes sit on concrete slab foundations, making engineered hardwood the preferred choice. The cross-layered construction handles moisture vapor from the slab and the humidity swings that Central Texas is known for. Our most popular engineered hardwood options for Cedar Park include:

  • White Oak — The dominant choice in Cedar Park right now. Clean grain, excellent stain absorption, and a Janka hardness of 1,360 that handles busy households. Wide-plank white oak in a natural or light grey stain is the look most Cedar Park homeowners are requesting.
  • Hickory — Perfect for Cedar Park families who want maximum durability. At Janka 1,820, hickory resists dents and scratches better than any other domestic species. Its dramatic grain variation adds rustic character to open-concept floor plans.
  • European Oak — A premium option popular in Cedar Park's higher-end neighborhoods. European oak features a tighter, more consistent grain than American white oak and is often available in wire-brushed or fumed finishes that add depth and texture.

For a full species comparison and Austin-specific recommendations, see our hardwood flooring Austin page and our guide on engineered vs solid hardwood.

The Cedar Park Installation Process

Every Cedar Park hardwood project follows our proven process:

  1. Free in-home consultation. We visit your Cedar Park home, measure each room, bring wood samples, and provide a detailed written estimate.
  2. Material acclimation. We deliver hardwood 5 to 7 days before installation so it adjusts to your home's temperature and humidity.
  3. Old flooring removal and subfloor prep. We remove carpet, tile, or existing flooring, test the slab for moisture, and ensure a flat, clean surface.
  4. Professional installation. Glue-down or floating method depending on the product and slab conditions. Tight seams, proper staggering, and expansion gaps throughout.
  5. Trim, transitions, and cleanup. We install baseboards and transitions, vacuum thoroughly, and walk the project with you to ensure satisfaction.
