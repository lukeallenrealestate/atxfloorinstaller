Hardwood Floor Installation in Pflugerville, TX

Pflugerville has grown from a small agricultural community into one of Austin's most dynamic suburbs, attracting families who want quality housing with easy access to downtown Austin and the tech corridor. ATX Floor Installer provides professional hardwood installation for Pflugerville homeowners in every neighborhood, from the established communities of Blackhawk and Highland Park to newer developments in Falcon Pointe, Avalon, and Villages of Hidden Lake.

Pflugerville's Growing Demand for Hardwood

Pflugerville's housing boom over the past two decades has created a large inventory of homes with builder-grade flooring that homeowners are ready to upgrade. The pattern is consistent: homes delivered with carpet in living areas and bedrooms, basic ceramic tile in bathrooms and entryways, and sometimes sheet vinyl or laminate in kitchens and laundry rooms. After 10 to 15 years, this flooring looks tired and worn.

Hardwood changes everything. It gives Pflugerville homes a premium, cohesive look that carries through the entire main level, eliminates the allergy-trapping properties of carpet (a real benefit during Austin's brutal cedar season), and adds significant resale value in a competitive market. Homes with hardwood in Pflugerville consistently appraise higher and sell faster than comparable properties with carpet or laminate.

Best Hardwood for Pflugerville Homes

Like most Austin-area cities, Pflugerville homes sit on concrete slab foundations. Engineered hardwood is the right choice here, offering the beauty of real wood with the dimensional stability needed to perform over concrete in Central Texas humidity conditions. Our top recommendations for Pflugerville:

  • White Oak — The most popular choice across all Pflugerville neighborhoods. Available in a wide range of stain colors from natural to dark walnut. Janka hardness 1,360, perfect for family households.
  • Hickory — Maximum durability at Janka 1,820. The dramatic grain variation adds character to the open floor plans common in Blackhawk and Falcon Pointe. An excellent choice for families with dogs.
  • Red Oak — A budget-friendly classic with warm undertones. Slightly softer than white oak but still very durable for residential use. Popular in Highland Park and the older sections of Pflugerville.

For a detailed species comparison, see our hardwood flooring Austin page. To compare hardwood with other options, read our hardwood vs LVP guide.

The Pflugerville Installation Process

  1. Free in-home consultation. We visit your Pflugerville home, measure rooms, discuss goals, and provide a written estimate with wood samples.
  2. Material acclimation. Hardwood delivered 5 to 7 days before installation to adjust to your home's conditions.
  3. Old flooring removal and subfloor prep. We remove existing carpet, tile, or laminate, test the slab for moisture, and ensure a level surface.
  4. Professional installation. Glue-down or floating method, with tight seams, proper staggering, and expansion gaps.
  5. Trim, transitions, and cleanup. Baseboards, transitions, thorough cleanup, and final walkthrough.

  • Solid hardwood (material + labor): $8 to $15 per square foot
