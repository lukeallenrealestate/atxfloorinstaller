Luxury Vinyl Plank Flooring in Georgetown, TX

Georgetown has emerged as one of the most desirable places to live in the Austin metro, attracting families, retirees, and professionals who appreciate its blend of historic charm and modern convenience. ATX Floor Installer provides professional LVP installation for Georgetown homes across every neighborhood, from the active adult community of Sun City to the golf course homes in Berry Creek and the growing suburbs of Wolf Ranch and Georgetown Village.

Why Georgetown Homeowners Love LVP

LVP has become Georgetown's fastest-growing flooring choice for good reason. It addresses the specific needs of this market better than almost any other material:

Sun City: Low-Maintenance Living

Sun City residents value ease of maintenance above almost everything else in a flooring material. LVP delivers exactly that. It never needs refinishing, resists scratches and stains with zero special treatment, and cleans up with a simple damp mop. For Sun City homeowners replacing worn carpet, LVP eliminates the allergens that carpet traps (a significant benefit during Austin's notorious cedar and oak pollen seasons) while providing a warm, wood-look surface that feels comfortable underfoot. The click-lock installation is also minimally disruptive, typically completing in 2 to 3 days for a standard Sun City floor plan.

Berry Creek: Golf Course Living

Berry Creek homes face unique flooring challenges. Golfers tracking in grass, mud, and moisture from the course need waterproof flooring that handles outdoor elements without damage. LVP's waterproof core means that muddy shoes, spilled drinks after a round, and the inevitable rain that gets tracked in through back doors are non-issues. Many Berry Creek homeowners install LVP throughout the main level and extend it into sunrooms and covered patios that connect to the course.

Wolf Ranch and Georgetown Village: New Construction Upgrades

Georgetown's newest communities along I-35 and Williams Drive feature homes built with builder-grade carpet and basic LVP or laminate. Homeowners in Wolf Ranch, Georgetown Village, and Serenada who want to step up from the standard package are choosing premium LVP with thicker wear layers, more realistic textures, and wider planks. The upgrade from builder-grade to premium LVP is one of the most cost-effective improvements available. See our guide on flooring for new construction.

Best LVP Products for Georgetown

  • SPC core with 20-mil+ wear layer. The rigid stone polymer composite core handles Georgetown's concrete slab foundations and temperature variations perfectly. Twenty-mil wear layers provide the scratch resistance needed for active households.
  • Wide-plank formats. Seven-inch-wide by 48-inch-long planks are the most popular choice in Georgetown, creating a realistic wood look that complements both traditional and modern interiors.
  • Light to medium tones. Georgetown homeowners tend to prefer natural oak, warm grey, and honey tones that brighten interiors and match the neutral paint palettes common in Sun City and Wolf Ranch homes.

For quality comparisons, read our LVP vs laminate guide and hardwood vs LVP comparison.

LVP Costs in Georgetown

  • Budget LVP (material + labor): $4 to $6 per square foot
  • Mid-range LVP (material + labor): $6 to $8 per square foot
  • Premium LVP (material + labor): $8 to $10 per square foot
