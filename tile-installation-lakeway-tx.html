Tile Installation in Lakeway, TX

Lakeway's hill country setting and lakeside lifestyle create unique opportunities for tile work that goes beyond standard kitchen and bathroom floors. ATX Floor Installer provides professional tile installation for Lakeway homes, including luxury bathroom builds, outdoor living areas, pool surrounds, and kitchen remodels. From natural stone showers in Rough Hollow estates to outdoor porcelain patios in Flintrock Falls, we deliver tile work that enhances both the beauty and functionality of Lakeway living.

Outdoor Tile for Lakeway Living

Lakeway homes are built for outdoor entertaining. Expansive covered patios, outdoor kitchens with built-in grills and pizza ovens, pool decks, and terraced hillside seating areas are standard features in neighborhoods like Rough Hollow and Flintrock. Outdoor tile elevates these spaces:

  • Pool deck and surround tile. Outdoor-rated porcelain with textured anti-slip surfaces and low water absorption creates a beautiful, safe surface around pools and spas. We select tile that handles full sun exposure, freeze-thaw cycles, and chlorine splash without fading or deteriorating.
  • Covered patio and outdoor kitchen floors. Large-format porcelain in natural stone patterns extends the indoor aesthetic to outdoor living spaces. Properly installed with exterior-grade mortar and grout, this tile handles temperature extremes and rain without cracking.
  • Hillside terraces and pathways. Lakeway's hilly terrain often includes terraced outdoor spaces with stairs and walkways connecting different levels. Textured porcelain or natural stone pavers provide both beauty and safe footing on these sloped surfaces.

Bathroom Tile Upgrades in Lakeway

Lakeway bathrooms are among the most ambitious tile projects we take on. Homeowners in this market expect spa-quality results:

Walk-In Shower Builds

Floor-to-ceiling tile with curbless entries, linear drains, rainfall and handheld showerheads, and built-in niches. We install full waterproofing membrane systems, ensure proper slope to drains, and execute precise tile work with tight grout lines. Materials range from large-format porcelain slabs to marble and natural stone mosaics. See our bathroom flooring page.

Heated Bathroom Floors

Electric radiant heat beneath porcelain or marble tile is one of Lakeway's most popular bathroom upgrades. We install systems with programmable thermostats that warm the floor before you step out of bed on winter mornings. Adds $4 to $8 per square foot to the tile installation.

Tub Surrounds and Feature Walls

Decorative tile accent walls behind freestanding tubs, mosaic feature strips in showers, and coordinated vanity backsplashes create cohesive bathroom designs that feel custom and intentional.

Kitchen Tile in Lakeway

Lakeway kitchens are designed for entertaining, and tile plays a central role:

  • Floor tile. Large-format porcelain in stone-look or concrete-look patterns creates a sleek, durable floor that handles the demands of cooking and hosting. Many Lakeway homeowners extend kitchen tile into adjacent butler's pantries and wet bars.
  • Backsplash tile. From hand-glazed zellige behind the range to marble herringbone behind the sink, the backsplash defines the kitchen's design personality. We work with designers and homeowners to source and install the perfect material.

See our kitchen flooring guide for more options.

Tile Costs in Lakeway

  • Porcelain tile (material + labor): $10 to $18 per square foot
  • Natural stone (material + labor): $15 to $30 per square foot
  • Outdoor tile (material + labor): $12 to $22 per square foot
