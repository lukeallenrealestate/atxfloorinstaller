Tile Installation in Pflugerville, TX

Pflugerville homeowners know that bathrooms and kitchens are where tile truly shines. ATX Floor Installer provides professional tile installation throughout Pflugerville, handling everything from master bathroom remodels in Blackhawk to kitchen floor upgrades in Falcon Pointe and entryway tile in Avalon. Our experienced tile crew delivers precision work that transforms the wet areas and high-traffic zones of your Pflugerville home.

Bathroom Tile Upgrades in Pflugerville

The master bathroom is consistently the most popular tile project in Pflugerville homes. After 10 to 15 years, the basic ceramic tile that builders installed has chipped, the grout has stained, and the small-format layout looks outdated. Pflugerville homeowners are upgrading to modern designs that make their bathrooms feel like spa retreats:

  • Walk-in shower builds. Removing tub-shower combos and creating spacious walk-in showers with floor-to-ceiling porcelain tile, linear drains, and frameless glass. This is the number one bathroom upgrade in Pflugerville's two-story homes in Falcon Pointe and Avalon.
  • Large-format floor tile. Replacing old 12-by-12 ceramic with 12-by-24 or larger porcelain. Fewer grout lines, easier cleaning, and a much more contemporary appearance.
  • Feature walls. Adding a mosaic or patterned tile accent wall behind the vanity or inside the shower niche. This design element adds visual interest without requiring a full-room tile upgrade.
  • Heated tile floors. Electric radiant heating under bathroom tile provides warm floors during Central Texas winters. A luxury upgrade that Pflugerville homeowners love.

See our bathroom flooring page for complete options.

Kitchen Tile in Pflugerville

Pflugerville kitchens serve as the hub of family life, and the flooring needs to handle heavy traffic, spills, and dropped cookware. Tile options for Pflugerville kitchens include:

  • Wood-look porcelain planks. Get the warmth of a wood floor with the waterproof durability of porcelain. These planks (typically 6-by-36 or 8-by-48 inches) install in a staggered pattern that mimics real hardwood and extends beautifully into open-concept living areas.
  • Large-format porcelain. 24-by-24 or 12-by-24 porcelain in stone-look or concrete-look patterns creates a sleek, modern kitchen floor that complements the quartz and granite countertops popular in Pflugerville homes.
  • Backsplash tile. The most popular kitchen tile project by far. Subway tile, herringbone patterns, and hexagonal mosaics are the top choices in Pflugerville. A new backsplash can be installed in a single day and completely refreshes the look of a kitchen.

Visit our kitchen flooring guide for more details.

Entryway and Mudroom Tile

Pflugerville homes with two-story floor plans often have a formal entryway that sets the first impression for visitors. Upgrading from builder-grade ceramic to a decorative porcelain or natural stone entry floor creates an immediate wow factor. For mudrooms and laundry areas, practical porcelain in dark tones handles the dirt and moisture that comes with active family life.

Tile Costs in Pflugerville

  • Ceramic tile (material + labor): $6 to $10/sq ft
  • Porcelain tile (material + labor): $8 to $14/sq ft
  • Natural stone (material + labor): $12 to $25/sq ft
  • Backsplash: $15 to $30/sq ft
  • Old tile removal: $2 to $4/sq ft
