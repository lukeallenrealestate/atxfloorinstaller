Tile Installation in Westlake Hills, TX

Westlake Hills homes demand tile work that matches the caliber of their architecture. ATX Floor Installer provides custom tile installation for Westlake Hills residences, specializing in natural stone, large-format porcelain, designer mosaics, and luxury bathroom builds. From walk-in shower masterpieces in Rob Roy to heated marble floors in Davenport Ranch master suites, our tile work elevates the most discerning Westlake Hills interiors.

Natural Stone Tile for Westlake Hills

Natural stone is the material of choice in many Westlake Hills homes, bringing an organic warmth and one-of-a-kind character that manufactured tile cannot replicate. We work with the finest natural stone materials:

  • Marble — Carrara, Calacatta, and Statuario marble for master bathroom floors, shower walls, vanity surrounds, and fireplace hearths. Each slab is unique, and we carefully lay out veining patterns for visual continuity across large surfaces.
  • Travertine — A hill country classic. Travertine's warm earth tones complement the limestone architecture common in Westlake Hills. Available in honed, tumbled, and polished finishes for floors, entryways, and outdoor patios.
  • Limestone — Understated elegance for great room floors and entryways. Limestone's muted tones create a sophisticated backdrop that lets other design elements shine. Requires professional sealing to maintain appearance.
  • Slate — Dramatic color variation and natural texture make slate a bold choice for entryways, mudrooms, and covered outdoor living areas in Westlake Hills homes.

Luxury Bathroom Tile in Westlake Hills

The master bathroom is where Westlake Hills tile work truly shines. Our most requested bathroom tile projects include:

Custom Walk-In Showers

Floor-to-ceiling marble or porcelain slab walls, curbless entries with linear drains, rainfall showerheads, built-in seating, and recessed niches with accent tile or natural stone. We handle complete waterproofing, proper slope engineering, and precision tile work that turns the shower into the centerpiece of the bathroom. See our bathroom flooring page for more.

Heated Bathroom Floors

Electric radiant heating beneath marble or porcelain tile is one of the most requested luxury upgrades in Westlake Hills bathrooms. The system adds $4 to $8 per square foot to the installation but delivers a comfort that homeowners use daily. We install Schluter DITRA-HEAT and similar systems with precise thermostat placement and uniform heat distribution.

Freestanding Tub Surrounds

Many Westlake Hills bathrooms feature freestanding soaking tubs as a design focal point. The tile work surrounding these tubs, whether a feature wall behind the tub or a decorative floor pattern beneath it, requires careful planning and execution to create a cohesive, spa-like atmosphere.

Kitchen and Living Area Tile

Westlake Hills kitchens are designed for both cooking and entertaining, and the tile work needs to match that dual purpose:

  • Large-format porcelain floors. 24-by-48 or 32-by-32 porcelain slabs in natural stone patterns create a luxurious, seamless floor with minimal grout lines. These large formats work beautifully in the open-concept kitchen and great room layouts common in Westlake Hills.
  • Designer backsplashes. Hand-glazed zellige, marble mosaics, metallic accent tile, and custom patterns that coordinate with countertops and cabinetry. The backsplash is often the design statement that ties a Westlake Hills kitchen together.
  • Outdoor kitchen and patio tile. Westlake Hills outdoor living spaces with built-in grills, pizza ovens, and bar areas benefit from outdoor-rated porcelain or natural stone tile that handles sun, rain, and temperature extremes.

Tile Costs in Westlake Hills

  • Porcelain tile (material + labor): $10 to $18 per square foot
  • Natural stone (material + labor): $15 to $35 per square foot
  • Large-format porcelain slab (material + labor): $18 to $30 per square foot
  • Custom shower build (complete): $5,000 to $15,000
  • Designer backsplash: $20 to $50 per square foot
  • Heated floor system (add-on): $4 to $8 per square foot
